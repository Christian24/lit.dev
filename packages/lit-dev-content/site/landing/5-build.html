Build the best user experience.

Lit is lightning fast

Weighing in at just 5 kB (minified and compressed), Lit helps keep your bundle size small and your loading time short. And UI updates are lightning-fast, because Lit only re-renders the dynamic parts–no diffing required.

Lit is standards based

You’re in good hands with Lit. We’ve been involved with Web Components from day one and remain actively engaged in standards work. Every Lit feature is carefully designed with platform evolution in mind, helping you take full advantage of the modern web.

Lit is future-ready

By using the browser’s native component model, Lit minimizes lock-in and maximizes flexibility. You’ll be able to update to a major new version of Lit or migrate to another Web Components library one component at a time safely, without disrupting product development.
